# AXIOM 🚀

A centralized platform for students at Newton School of Technology to access academic resources, campus information, events, and opportunities — all in one place.

---

## 🌐 Overview

AXIOM acts as a **gateway to tech knowledge and campus life**, enabling students to easily navigate:

- Academic resources
- Clubs & societies
- Campus facilities
- Events & opportunities
- Mentorship and startups

---

## ✨ Features

- 📚 **Academics** – Courses, faculty, and learning resources  
- 🏫 **Campus Life** – Facilities and accommodation info  
- 🎉 **Events** – Tech fests and cultural activities  
- 👥 **Clubs & Societies** – Student communities  
- 🧑‍🏫 **Mentors** – Guidance and support  
- 🚀 **Startups** – Newton Startup Fund access  

---

## 🖥️ Tech Stack

- Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript (React/Next.js if applicable)  
- UI: Responsive, modern design  
- Deployment: Web-based  

---

## 🎯 Purpose

To create a **single source of truth** for NST students and simplify access to important resources.
